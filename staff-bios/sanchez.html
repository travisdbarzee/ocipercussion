Jesus Sanchez

Snare Consultant

Jesus Sanchez is an active performer and educator in both the marching arts and drum set idioms.

Jesus is currently the percussion caption head at the Phantom Regiment and also performs with the Disneyland Band in Anaheim, CA.

Jesus holds a B.M. in Jazz Studies from the California State University at Fullerton, a M.M. in Jazz Studies from the University of Southern California and is currently a D.M.A. candidate at USC.
