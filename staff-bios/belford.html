Will Belford

Tenor Instructor

Will Belford is a tenor drummer and instructor originally from Los Altos, California, and is going into his final year at UC Irvine. His marching experience includes contracts with Blue Devils B in 2019 and 2020 as well as Thesis Indoor in 2022, before long-term injuries unfortunately ended his marching career.

Even though his performing career was cut short, Will has been referred to numerous drum lines across California, and currently teaches multiple ensembles in Orange County, CA. Will aims to provide each and every student with the most positive and educational marching experience possible.
